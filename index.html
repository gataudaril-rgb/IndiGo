ABOUT

About India

India is a country with rich history, culture, and diverse architecture. It is known as one of the centers of ancient civilizations and has many historical legacies from various kingdoms and religions.

Its historic cities are a major attraction for international tourists. Some featured destinations in this project include New Delhi, Mumbai, Agra, and Jaipur.
